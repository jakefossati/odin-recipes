Brownies!

Description

Brownies! What's not to love. Give me some milk or ice cream and I'll be fine with brownies.

Ingredients

  • 1.5c sugar
  • 3/4c AP flour
  • 2/3c cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/2c pow sugar, sifted
  • 1/2c dark chocolate chips
  • 2 big ole eggs (large)
  • 1/2c canola oil
  • 2T water
  • 1/2t vanilla

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325, spray 8x8 bakin dish and line with parchment paper. Spray the paper too. Why not. Spray everything.
  2. In medium bowl, combine dry ingredients
  3. In large bowl, whisk wet ingredients together
  4. Sprinkle dry over mix, stir until just combined
  5. Pour batter into pan, smoooth top with spatula. Bake for 40-48 min until toothpick comes out mostly clean. Let cool and slice
