Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is tasty. It is fulfilling. And it is easy to make

It's so good, even Garfield eats it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2c minced onion
  • 28oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 8oz can tomato sauce
  • 2T chopped parsley, divided
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1.5t dried basil
  • 1.5t salt
  • 1/2t oregano
  • pinch sugar
  • 16 oz package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4c grated parm
  • 2t salt
  • heavy pinch pepper
  • 16oz package shredded mozz chz

Steps

  1. Mix meats and cook until browned. Add onion and cook until translucent
  2. Stir in both tomato items, herbs until sugar on the list. Turn down heat and simmer 30 min, stirring occasionally
  3. While sauce simmers, cook pasta in boiling salted water
  4. While noodles cook, preheat oven to 375 deg F
  5. Mix everything else other than the shredded mozz in a bowl
  6. Assemble lasagna as Follows
    • Sauce
    • Noodles
    • Cheese Mixture
    • Repeat until full, top with shredded mozz, cover with foil
  7. Bake for like 35 more min idk
